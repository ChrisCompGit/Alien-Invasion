Welcome.

To play the game, use the “LEFT” and “RIGHT” arrow keys to move the cannon and press the “SPACEBAR” to shoot.

Spaceships will appear at the top of the screen with an equation that you must solve using the answer displayed on the cannon.

The spaceships will descend on the cannon. If a spaceship hits the cannon or the floor, the game will end.

When you shoot the correct spaceship, all spaceships will reset to their original
position and the questions displayed on them will also reset, along with the answer on the cannon.

When you shoot an incorrect spaceship, the questions and answer will reset,
but the spaceships will continue to descend from their current position.

All “Hits” and “Misses” are displayed at the top of the screen and for every hit, you gain 100 points.

When the timer reaches zero, you will advance to the next level.

Level 1 = Addition

Level 2 = Subtraction
